Foe Yong Hai

Description

Foeyonghai is a Chinese egg dish. In the Netherlands it is often fried as an omelet and eaten with sweet and sour tomato sauce and rice. According to the traditional recipe, foeyonghai is made with crab, hai means crab.

    For the omelet:

  • 5 eggs
  • juice of 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • salt
  • pepper

    For the filling:

  • 200 grams of shrimp
  • 2 to 3 slices of shrimp
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • the yellow part of 2 leeks
  • 4 to 5 small cooked carrots
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons green peas
  • 2 tablespoons tomato ketchup

    Step by Step

  1. Sauté the bacon in the oil, add the sliced ​​garlic and leek and sauté with the shrimps.
  2. Stir in the peas and the tomato ketchup.
  3. Cook the omelet on both sides, fill it, double-fold it and cover it with one of the ginger sauces (see Chapter Sauces).
